# Liqdx / Arc AI Agentic Treasury

## Overview
**Name**: Liqdx / AI Agentic Treasury
## Identity & Positioning 
**Definitive Classification**: Liqdx is an **Intent-Powered Portfolio & Cross-Chain Agent**. 

Why this definition?
- **Portfolio Management**: It tracks your net worth, assets, and balances across multiple chains seamlessly.
- **Cross-Chain Agent**: Unlike a passive portfolio tracker, it actively executes swaps and bridges across networks based on your natural language commands.
- **The Complete Package**: It combines passive tracking (portfolio) with active execution (agent) into one single platform.

## Core Features
- **AI Agent Execution**: Users can input natural language commands (e.g., "Swap 0.1 USDC" or "Bridge 0.1 USDC") and the AI will parse the intent and execute the real on-chain transaction.
- **Cross-Chain Operations**: Support for Swapping and Bridging assets across multiple networks.
- **Autonomous Vault**: Users can deposit funds into an autonomous vault and withdraw at any time.
- **Multi-Chain Support**: Integrates with Ethereum, Solana, Base, Arbitrum, Arc, Sepolia, and Avalanche.
- **Supported Assets**: USDC, EURC, USDT, ETH, and native tokens.
- **AI Credits System**: Operations require "AI Credits" to execute, implying a tokenized or credit-based usage model.
- **Transaction & Chat History**: Keeps a full ledger of chat sessions and past on-chain transactions for transparent auditing.
- **Twitter Authentication**: Allows users to link their Twitter profiles (OAuth) to their wallet identities.



## Example Use Cases (Intents)
1. **Swap**: Exchanging one stablecoin or asset for another (e.g., USDC to EURC).
2. **Bridge**: Moving assets seamlessly from one supported chain to another (e.g., Base to Arbitrum).
3. **Balance Query**: Asking the AI to fetch net worth across all connected chains.
